About Benson

Benson Dunwoody is a gumball machine, and the manager of The Park. He is the boss of every worker but Pops, and despite his temper, he sometimes shows his kinder side.

Benson with his friends Mordecai and Rigby

Benson's Characteristics

  1. He's got a short temper
  2. He's in charge of nearly everyone
  3. He does care about his worker
